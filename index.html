Hello there! 
HTCNA (Holy Trinity Community of North America) finally get a website started. 
We dedicated this website to help our Holy Trinity Community members as well as those who might be interested to be a part of Holy Trinity Community to become more familiar and involved in our community.

Holy Trinity Community consist of a group of people of God, who together, desire to reach a mature Christian Life. The purpose of the Christian life is written in Mark 12:30 "You shall love the Lord your God with all your heart, with all your soul, with all your mind, and with all your strength". This Community was founded on January 11, 1987 during a retreat in Ngadireso, East Java, Indonesia by father Yohanes Indrakusuma, O'Carm, who previously has founded 2 religious communities, Kongregasi Putri Karmel (Daughters of Carmel) for women and CSE (Carmelitae Sancti Eliae) for men. The new community is named Holy Trinity Community so that the members will always, reflect on the great mystery of Love between the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit and strive to experience it also.

Diberi nama Komunitas Tritunggal Mahakudus agar para anggotanya mampu untuk selalu mengenangkan misteri cinta agung antara Bapa, Putera dan Roh Kudus, serta berusaha menghayatinya.

Komunitas Tritunggal Mahakudus bertekad menjadi tanda bagi nilai-nilai yang mengatasi dunia, tanda pengharapan bagi yang putus asa, tanda penghiburan bagi yang berduka dan menderita, serta penuntun pada perjumpaan dengan Allah yang hidup.
